About Me

I, MAJ (FR) JESSIE B GECAIN CHS, was ordained priest last June 10, 2008, by the late His Eminence Ricardo J Cardinal Vidal, D.D. at Cebu Metropolitan Cathedral. At that time, I belonged to Maryâ€™s Little Children Community (MLCC). On March 2009, I left MLCC after it suffered a crisis and joined the splinter-group, the Community of the Salt and Light of Christ (CSLC). While at CSLC, I had served as a missionary priest in the Diocese of Sibu, Sarawak, Malaysia, from March 2009 until our community was dissolved last April 2, 2014. While serving in the Diocese of Sibu, I was designated by Most Rev Joseph Hii Teck Kwong, Bishop of the Diocese of Sibu as Chairman of the Commission of Social Communications and Mass Media.

Prior for my re-entry program in the Archdiocese of Cebu, I had finished my Certificate Program in Filmmaking on October 25, 2014 at the International Academy of Film and Television, Mactan, Cebu. On December 2014, I was sent to Bantayan Island for the start of my re-entry program under the guidance of Msgr Vicente Rey Penagunda and supervision of Msgr Alfredo Romanillos. While being there, I was able to make three short films, uploaded in my YouTube Channel Direk Among, namely, "Binilanggo sa Kagahapon", "When Her Rights Went Wrong" and "Dinagkotang Pangandoy".

During the 51st International Eucharistic Congress in Cebu, I met the PAF Chaplain delegates, and was recruited to join the Chaplain Service. With the endorsement of Bishop Leopoldo S Tumulak D.D., and the recommendation of Archbishop Jose S Palma, D.D., thanks be to God, I am now serving as PAF Chaplain under your paternal care and guidance of the MOST REVEREND OSCAR JAIME L FLORENCIO, D.D, SThD, Bishop of the Military Ordinariate of the Philippines.

At present, I am now the Base Chaplain of 540th Air Base Group, Basa Air Base, Floridablanca, Pampanga. I serve here since March 2022. Since then, with the help of the Chapel Pastoral Council, I have done four short films, namely: "Munting Dalangin, Munting Pangarap," "Sapagkat Tao Lamang," "Mga Mukha ng Pag-ibig," and "Sa Kandungan ng Nanay".

With my previous experiences in the field of Social Communications and Mass Media, and with my interest and basic skills in filmmaking, I now explore my interests in Web Development, to hopefully serve better in the area of Social Communications.

May Almighty God bless my every endeavour for our good and for His Greater Glory. Amen.

Thank you very much.
